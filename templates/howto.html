Example Pianobar eventcmd script

Fill this in and place it in ~/.config/pianobar/eventcmd.

You can download this script by copying and pasting, or clicking the "raw" link and saving the file, or by viewing the gist on Github and clicking the download button. Back up ~/.config/pianobar/eventcmd, then put this script in its place. Make sure that you fill in your Google username and Scrobbular secret, then now playing and scrobbles will be posted to Last.fm through Scrobbular.
