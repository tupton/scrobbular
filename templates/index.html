What is Scrobbular?

Scrobbular makes it easy to scrobble songs to Last.fm from any application.

First, you need to allow Scrobbular to scrobble songs in your name. Enter your Last.fm username and click Authenticate. You will be taken to a Last.fm page that allows you to give the necessary permission to Last.fm. If you are not logged in to Last.fm, you will be asked to log in first.
